Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • 🍝 250g Fettuccine Pasta
  • 🧄 3 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 🥛 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 🧀 1 cup Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  • 🧈 4 tbsp Butter
  • 🌿 Salt & Black Pepper to taste
  • 🌱 Fresh Parsley (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add heavy cream and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until smooth and creamy.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper.
  6. Add cooked pasta into the sauce and toss well.
  7. Garnish with parsley and extra cheese. Serve warm!

Notes

You can add grilled chicken, mushrooms, or shrimp to make it even tastier. Use freshly grated Parmesan for the best flavor!
